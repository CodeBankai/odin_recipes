Homemade Masala Maggi

Learn how to make maggi from a professional. In the industry since birth.

Ingredients

  • Maggi with masala
  • Water 2 cups
  • Salt (according to taste)

Steps

  1. heat water till a boil
  2. now on high heat, add your masala and stir to mix it well, add salt according to taste.
  3. Immediately add your noodles and start stirring to prevent burning from bottom.
  4. Keep gently stirring till most of the water is dried off.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!!
