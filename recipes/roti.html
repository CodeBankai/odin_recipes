Homemade roti

Nothing to read. Just a simple roti recipe

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add 250gms of wheat flour and all 100 ml of water.
  2. Knead the dough until a elastic consistency.
  3. Now roll the dough and cut it into small pieces of balls
  4. now flatten the dough and cut circles into it with a big plate.
  5. Now take the circles and cook them under flame for sometime till you observe the dough rising and fluffing up.
  6. Serve hot and Enjoy!!!
